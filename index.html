About Me

I’m Sriram T, a Cybersecurity Engineer and versatile creative professional. I completed my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and Engineering specialising in Cyber Security on 2025. After that, I started learning about graphic design and web development. And my passion is photography and editing. So I started as a Freelancer to provide services in Graphic Designing, Website Development, Photography, Cinematography and Editing. To be frank, it started for survival instinct.

My academic background and hands-on projects such as building an Intrusion Detection & Prevention System (IDPS) and developing DoS attack detection tools—reflect my commitment to strengthening security frameworks. With a strong foundation in network security, penetration testing, incident response and threat intelligence, I specialize in safeguarding digital infrastructures using tools like Splunk, Wazuh, Wireshark, Nessus and BurpSuite.

Beyond technology, I channel my creativity into freelancing services including photography, cinematography, website development and graphic design. Whether it’s capturing cinematic visuals, designing modern websites or crafting digital art, I bring precision and storytelling into every project.

Driven by curiosity, adaptability and analytical thinking, I thrive at the intersection of technology and art—securing networks while creating impactful visuals and designs.
